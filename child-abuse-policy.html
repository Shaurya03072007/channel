Child Safety Policy

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY V9 News maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any content that facilitates child exploitation or endangerment. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users.

1. Prohibited Content

We strictly prohibit the following:

  • Any content depicting the sexual exploitation or abuse of minors.
  • Content that promotes or facilitates child grooming or harmful contact.
  • Any imagery or text that endangers the physical or emotional well-being of children.
  • Distribution of links to external sites containing prohibited material.

2. Reporting Mechanisms

If you encounter any content that violates this policy, you must report it immediately using the "Report" feature within the app. Alternatively, you can contact our dedicated safety team at:

Email: safety@v9news.com

3. Technical Safeguards

V9 News employs advanced automated systems and manual moderation to detect and remove prohibited content. We utilize hash-matching technology (such as PhotoDNA) to identify known CSAM and prevent its distribution.

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

V9 News complies with all legal obligations, including reporting suspected CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperating with global law enforcement agencies in criminal investigations.

5. Compliance with Laws

Our platform is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant regional safety regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent.
